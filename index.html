WHY WAIT TO CELEBRATE

After almost 8 years we are thrilled to announce our engagement and would like to invite you to join us in celebrating this joyous occasion. The festivities will take place at Boatrocker Brewers & Distillers (Braeside, Victoria) on the 11th of November 2023 at 12pm.

If the last 3 years have taught us anything its that we don't know what the future holds, we want to celebrate our engagement with our loved ones now, whilst we continue to plan our happily ever after for the Spring of 2025. üòä

Events

Join us for a series of relaxed get-togethers in the days leading up to, and after the engagement party, as outlined below. To help us ensure we have the right reservations in place, kindly inform Joshua or Kahlia of your interest in attending any or all of these celebration events.

9th November

Trivia Night 7PM - 9PM

Join us for Trivia at Boatrocker hosted by Ben Soresen. Trivia starts at 7pm and usually runs until about 9pm. There are always drink and food specials including $10 G&Ts you wont regret... but if you can't make it, we will be back at Boatrocker on Saturday for the main event.

10th November

Dinner at Liberty üóΩ7PM - 9PM

Hailing from Helensville in Auckland, New Zealand, Liberty has opened their first Australian tap room. Join us for a casual drink and some dinner (I believe its Vietnamese on Fridays).

11th November

Engagement Party 12PM-4PM

The main event, - Do we need to say more...
Following the celebrations at Boatrocker we will be kicking on to two other venues.

Session at Braeside 4PM-6PM

Braeside is another local brewery, walking distance from Boatrocker it will be stop number 1. Braeside always has a great selection of beer fresh on tap, it was founded in 2018 and produces over 1500 cans of beer a week from their brewhouse inside the tap room. It is a must have stop on the way.

Dinner at Mates 6PM-Late

We'll finish the day at mates. Bar & Bottleshop. With a large range of beverages in fridges, on tap, and food nearby, it will send off the night nicely.
